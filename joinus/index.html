Join EcoAI Lab

We are always looking for motivated students and researchers to join our team!

Open Positions

  1. PhDs - We are recruiting PhD students interested in ML systems, computer architecture, and efficient ML algorithms.

  2. Interns - Research opportunities are available for master’s and undergraduate students who want to gain research experience.

How to Apply

If you are interested in joining our lab, please send an email with the following materials:

  1. Your CV/Resume
  2. Academic transcripts
  3. A brief description of your research interests and experience
  4. [Optional] Any relevant publications or project reports

I apologize ahead of time if I don’t have the bandwidth to reply to every inquiry email, but we’ll make sure to review everyone’s profile.

For prospective PhDs, please also apply through the official Purdue system, following the guidance here.
